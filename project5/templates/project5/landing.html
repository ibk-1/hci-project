Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback
About this page

This tool demonstrates Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback (RLHF) using a toy mouse-and-cheese world.

  • You will see two possible trajectories (A and B) of a mouse moving through a grid with walls, traps, normal cheese, and organic cheese.
  • Each trajectory has a return score (how well the mouse performed).
  • Your task is to pick the better trajectory by clicking Prefer A or Prefer B. For example, prefer the one with fewer traps or better cheese.
  • Click Load New Pair to see another set of trajectories and continue labeling.
  • After collecting enough preferences, go to the Trainer page (via the top menu) to:
    • Train a baseline policy (REINFORCE)
    • Train a reward model from your labels
    • Fine-tune the policy with RLHF
  • This shows how human feedback (your choices) can guide a reinforcement learning agent to improve over time.

This app trains a mouse to find cheese. We learn a policy with REINFORCE (Task 1), then refine it with human preferences using a Bradley–Terry reward model and a KL penalty (Tasks 2–3).
